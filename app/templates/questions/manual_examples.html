About Manual Examples

Creating high-quality manual examples helps the model learn your preferred question style and format.

Tips for Good Training Examples:
  • Use clear, concise context paragraphs
  • Write questions that directly relate to the context
  • Provide specific, accurate answers
  • Mix different question types and complexity levels
  • Create at least 5-10 examples per file for better results

After creating examples, go to the Training page to train the model.
